## What is ORION?

[**<u>ORION</u>**](www.oriorion.com) is a process automation and intelligent business orchestration platform designed for the Agentic AI era. It empowers enterprises to build business systems with intelligent perception, autonomous decision-making, and continuous evolution capabilities.

At its core, ORION features a powerful business orchestration engine derived from the open-source Camunda project. Building on Camunda’s mature and reliable process execution capabilities, ORION extends its architecture to support the needs of enterprise digital transformation and intelligent evolution.

As a practical implementation of the BOAT capabilities (Business Orchestration and Automation Technology) proposed by Gartner, ORION deeply integrates process orchestration, low-code application development, AI-assisted design, and Agentic intelligence. It provides business and technical teams with a unified platform that is collaborative, extensible, trustworthy, and intelligently adaptive.

## Core Features of ORION

Design, Run, Experience, and Continuously Optimize!

#### Design: From Idea to Process

- Supports BPMN 2.0 and DMN 1.3 standards for process and decision modeling
- Drag-and-drop interface for building process, agent, forms, pages, and data models
- Use the AI Copilot Assistant to generate scripts, expressions, and rules using natural language

#### Run: Let Your Business Processes Truly Flow

- Built-in Task Center for clear task assignment and business execution
- Full process monitoring with support for both automation and human collaboration
- Supports Agent orchestration, enabling automatic intent recognition and task execution

#### Experience: A Truly Usable Process System

- Built-in Task Center for clear task assignment and business execution
- 应用中心集中访问所有业务流程应用
- Copilot助手随时提供智能建议与自动操作，提升效率

## 设计并运行你的第一个ORION流程

### 设计一个流程

在 ORION 中，设计流程是一项直观且高效的操作。请按照以下步骤完成你的第一个业务流程设计：

1. 进入设计中心：从系统主导航中点击 **设计中心**（Design Studio）入口，进入流程应用的设计工作区。
2. 创建一个项目：点击右上角的 **新建项目** 按钮，输入项目名称与描述。每个项目用于组织相关的流程与资源。

![](assets/20250728_114114_newproject.png)

3. 创建一个业务流程：在项目中，点击 **新建流程**。你可以为流程设置名称、版本号，并选择流程类型（如普通流程、审批流程等）。

![](assets/20250728_114149_newprocess.png)

4. 绘制业务流程：进入流程编辑器后，你可以通过拖拽节点的方式绘制BPMN业务流程图。
5. 定义流程ID：流程 ID 是流程在系统内部的唯一标识。你可以在流程属性面板中自定义流程 ID，建议使用具有语义的命名方式，例如：**Process_employee_onboarding**

![](assets/20250728_114308_designprocess.png)

6. 保存流程：流程设计完成后，点击顶部工具栏的 **保存** 按钮，流程即被保存到当前项目中。你可以继续编辑、发布或测试此流程。

### 与团队的协作

你可以通过以下两种方式，与他人协同完成流程设计：

1. **生成分享链接**
   将流程图以链接形式分享给外部人员，点击即可查看，无需登录。

![](assets/20250728_114725_share.png)

2. **邀请系统用户协作**
   将其他系统用户添加为协作者，共同编辑和管理流程项目。

![](assets/20250728_114833_invite.png)
